How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about Campus Connect

general How do I create an account on Campus Connect? ▼
To create an account, click the Sign Up button, fill in your details, and verify your email.
general How can I find a mentor? ▼
You can find a mentor by visiting the Mentor section on your dashboard and browsing available mentors.
admissions What is the admission process? ▼
The admission process involves submitting your application, attending an interview, and receiving acceptance.
career How do I apply for internships? ▼
You can apply for internships by visiting the Career section and browsing available opportunities.
